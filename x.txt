Sustainable Development Goals
A healthy environment plays a key role in meeting many of the 17 Sustainable Development Goals.
With a little over 10 years left to meet the target date of 2030, the world will need to pick up the pace and put greater efforts in finding better solutions to pollution, climate change and biodiversity loss in order to truly transform societies and economies.
